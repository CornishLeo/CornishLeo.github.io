About the App

Keskow is an innovative language practice app designed to help users improve their language skills through interactive exercises and advanced language models. By leveraging AI technology, Keskow ensures a personalized experience that keeps you engaged.

Features

  • Personalized Learning: Tailors lessons to your proficiency level.
  • Interactive Exercises: Engaging activities to enhance vocabulary and grammar.
  • Progress Tracking: Monitor your improvement and set learning goals.
  • AI Conversations: Practice speaking with a smart AI that simulates real-life conversations.

Screenshots

Technologies Used

Keskow is built using a combination of the following technologies:

  • Frontend: Flutter (Dart)
  • Backend: Firebase Authentication and Vertex AI (Gemini)
  • AI Technology: Gemini 1.5 Flash
  • Database: Firestore by Firebase

Future Plans

We aim to expand Keskow by adding more languages, improving AI capabilities, and incorporating user feedback to enhance the learning experience. Stay tuned for future updates!
